Curriculum Overview
What You Will
Master

A structured 21-lecture journey through every aspect of sewer network design — from flow fundamentals to complete system output.

01
Sewerage Fundamentals
Types of sewerage systems, CPHEEO guidelines, design philosophy, and Indian urban drainage context.
02
Population Forecasting
Arithmetic, geometric, and incremental increase methods. Forecasting design populations for 30-year planning horizons.
03
Wastewater Flow Estimation
Per capita generation, peak factor, infiltration allowance, and calculating design discharge for each reach.
04
Hydraulic Design (Manning's)
Manning's equation applied to circular sewers, partial flow diagrams, velocity and depth of flow checks.
05
Sewer Sizing & Slopes
Minimum and maximum velocities, self-cleansing criteria, pipe diameter selection, and slope determination.
06
Network Layout & Excel Tool
Full sewer network design using a structured Excel workbook with 1,000+ live formulas — ready for real projects.
